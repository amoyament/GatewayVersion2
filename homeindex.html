Welcome to The Robin's Nest!

Fly on into The Robin's Nest Café whenever you're feeling peckish. The café was opened by Victor and Evelyn Brooks in 1964 right here in the heart of Matthews. It has remained a town staple ever since. This cozy cafe is everyone's favorite favorite breakfast spot. Some menu favorites include the Cinnamon Swirl Pancakes, Florentine Omlet, and Sweet Heat Chicken & French Toast Bites. However the crowd favorite still is and always has been The Nest Blend house coffee. So simple yet so delicious contines to be the motto of every item this menu. This, paired with the homey feel that permeates every aspect of the place is what makes it truly unique. Evelyn reminds every patron she sees that when you're at her family's cafe, you will always be part of the nest.
